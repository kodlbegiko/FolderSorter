# Privacy

FolderSorter is local-first.

- Files are scanned and organized on your Mac.
- Rules are stored locally in `UserDefaults` or exported JSON files you choose.
- Undo transactions are stored locally under `~/Library/Application Support/FolderSorter/Transactions`.
- The app does not upload files, filenames, rules, or analytics.
- The current version has no network feature.

If future AI or cloud features are added, they should be opt-in, clearly labeled,
and disabled by default.
